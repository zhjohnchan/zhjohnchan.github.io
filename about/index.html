About

Zhihong Chen is a first-year Ph.D. student (expected to graduate around July 2024) at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen. He is supervised jointly by Prof. Tsung-Hui Chang and Prof. Xiang Wan.

He received the B.Eng. degree from Sun Yat-sen University in 2019, and then became a PhD student at SSE, CUHKSZ and SRIBD. His research interests include natural language processing and deep learning, particularly focusing on medical big data.
